Recipe 1

Description goes here.

  • Time to Bake: 20 minutes.
  • Serves: 4 people.

Ingredients:

  • 200g Baking Soda
  • 250g Flour
  • 5 Glowsticks

Steps:

  1. Add the baking soda and flour to a large pot. Stir.

  2. After five minutes of stirring the powder, break the glowsticks and add them into the pot.

  3. Add 500g of water from the tap.

  4. Bake for 20 minutes in the oven.

  5. Serve to your guests when hot and run for the hills.
